Unlucky man struck by lightning, frying wining lottery ticket

Today a man won the loterry in Detroit, Michigan. The man named Bryan Logan bought a scratch off ticket that was worth $100,000 as he was exiting the shop he was struck by lighting frying the ticket and causing serious harm to his health. Mr. Logan is currently in the hospital recovering and is in stable condition. We were able to get a statement from him. "This is god punishing me for my sins, I wish for death but he will not give it to me. Why must I go on like this." It seems Mr. Logan is in good health and good spirits!
